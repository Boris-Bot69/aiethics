AI Ethics Scenarios

Explore real-world AI ethics scenarios that challenge you to think critically about the ethical implications of artificial intelligence in various contexts.

Scenario 1: AI in Healthcare

A hospital is considering implementing an AI system to prioritize emergency room patients. The system uses historical data to predict patient outcomes, but the training data may contain biases. How should the hospital address potential fairness issues?

Scenario 2: AI in Education

An AI tutoring system personalizes learning content based on student behavior and performance. However, it may inadvertently reinforce stereotypes or limit students' exposure to diverse perspectives. What ethical considerations should guide its development?

Scenario 3: AI in Criminal Justice

An AI system is used to assess the likelihood of a defendant reoffending. The system's recommendations influence sentencing decisions. How can we ensure the system is fair and doesn't perpetuate existing biases in the criminal justice system?

Scenario 4: AI in Social Media

Social media platforms use AI algorithms to curate content and target advertisements. These systems can create echo chambers and spread misinformation. What responsibilities do tech companies have in addressing these ethical concerns?

Each scenario includes discussion questions, ethical frameworks to consider, and resources for further exploration. Use these scenarios to practice applying AI ethics principles to real-world situations.
