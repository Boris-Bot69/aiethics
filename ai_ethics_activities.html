AI Ethics Activities

The scenarios encourage conversations about AI ethics related to the OECD AI principles. Each scenario contains conversation prompts associated with three of the OECD AI Ethics Principles to facilitate discussions about the relevance of AI ethics and to foster AI ethics literacy.
