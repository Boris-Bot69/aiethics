Magazine Cut-outs

In this activity, you will create artwork, upload it to edit with AI, print it, and repeat this process various times. You will create a simple black and white image, and upload it to an AI to edit one or two things from it, and then print it and to layer magazine cutouts on it. Then take a picture, edit it again, and print this one as well. Showcase the four artworks to visualize the editing process. By reflecting on how your art changes when mixed with magazine cutouts and AI, you will engage with ethics principles. You can do this alone, with a partner, or in a small group.

Activity Instructions

1. Create simple artwork:

Use the art materials to draw something you like, only in black and white. Keep it simple.

2. Edit with AI:

Upload your artwork and edit one or two things with the help of AI. Print the output.

3. Edit with magazines:

Use magazines to cut out pieces to layer on top of your drawing. Take your time to add details to reimagine your drawing.

4. Edit with AI:

Upload your layered cutout and now edit it with AI. Play around with it and notice how the AI interprets your initial drawing and the magazine cutout layered one.

5. Exhibit and share:

Display your four artworks side by side and connect them like a flowchart, explaining what changed with each iteration. Share your process with the group.

6. Reflect:

Use the discussion prompts to guide the conversation about ethics.

Guiding Questions for AI Ethics Literacy

Creativity and human capabilities
  • Creativity: Between adding magazine cutouts by hand and having the AI edit your drawing, which process felt more creative?
  • Human capabilities: In which process did you feel your own capabilities were enhanced? In which did you feel they were replaced?
Fairness and agency
  • Agency: In which steps did you have the most control over the final image? Who is the author of each artwork?
Transparency and explainability
  • Outcomes: Were the outputs of the AI surprising? If so, does that make the AI more creative?
Robustness and safety
  • Robustness: Did the AI produce a coherent image, or did it generate strange errors? Did the tool operate in the way you expected?
  • Misuse: Could this tool be used in harmful ways?
