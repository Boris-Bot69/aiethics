Expanded frames

In this activity, you will create artwork and expand its frame with AI. You will iterate through co-creation with AI to make the artwork larger. In the activity you will expand an A8 artwork into an A4, but you can expand as much as you want. By observing how AI fills the artwork beyond its borders, you will engage with ethics principles. You can do this alone, with a partner, or in a small group.

Activity Instructions

1. Create an artwork:

Create an original artwork using the materials available on an A8-sized paper. It could be anything; donâ€™t limit yourself! Once you are done, take a picture of it.

2. Expand the frame with AI:

Now, use this picture and expand it with AI. The expansion should be an A7 size.

3. Expand with materials:

Now you expand it to an A6 size.

4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until you reach A4 size

5. Share:

Present your finalized A4 artwork and share the different frame iterations in an art exhibition that details the progress of the original artwork to the expanded ones.

6. Reflect:

Use the discussion prompts to guide the conversation about ethics.

Materials

  • A8 - A4 sizes or frames
  • Cardstock
  • Pens and colored pencils

Guiding Questions for AI Ethics Literacy

Creativity and human capabilities
  • Creativity: Comparing your and the AI expansions, which are more creative, interesting, or surprising?
  • Human capabilities: Did the AI's expansion inspire new ideas for your own turn?
Fairness and agency
  • Agency: Who is the final author of the A4 artwork? How much of the final piece was guided by human decisions versus AI decisions? At what point, if any, did it stop feeling like your artwork?
  • Data: Is the AI good at expanding your style? What does this say about its training data?
Transparency and explainability
  • Outcomes: When the AI expanded your artwork, could you explain why it added what it did?
  • Understanding: Did you feel like you were collaborating with a partner that understood your art or a tool that followed a set of rules?
Robustness and safety
  • Robustness: How well did the AI handle your inputs? Did it produce glitches or errors in the expansions?
Accountability
  • Responsibility: What responsibility do the creators of the AI tool have for the images it generates? What features could they build to ensure accountability?
