What is AI?

AI systems leverage advanced machine learning from vast datasets to generate diverse content (text, images, videos, audio) and can process multi-modal data to engage in natural conversations.

What is AI ethics?

AI ethics is a critical field that addresses the implications of artificial intelligence in everyday life such as in smartphones or search results. It grapples with ethical concerns such as biases, privacy, and misuse.

What is AI ethics literacy?

Practices that foster a process of deliberating implications of AI on what's being learned and how, in relation to the AI ethics principles, as well as knowing the actors involved and their relationships (e.g., who is responsible and who is affected).
