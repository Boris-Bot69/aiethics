OECD Guiding Questions

The OECD AI principles codebook provides a guide for educators and practitioners to identify AI ethics in action. The codebook was developed through the analysis of youth workshops on developing AI ethics literacy.

Principle 1: Inclusive Growth

AI should benefit people and the planet by driving inclusive growth, sustainable development, and well-being.

Principle 2: Human-Centered Values

AI systems should be designed to respect human rights, democratic values, and diversity.

Principle 3: Transparency

AI systems should be transparent and explainable, enabling people to understand AI-based outcomes.

Principle 4: Robustness & Safety

AI systems should function in a robust, secure, and safe way throughout their life cycles.

These principles serve as a foundation for developing AI ethics literacy and guide decision-making in AI development and deployment.
